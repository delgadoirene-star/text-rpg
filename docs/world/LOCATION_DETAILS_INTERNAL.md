# INTERNAL / SPOILERS — Location Details

**Audience:** Developers, writers, designers.

This document contains detailed location descriptions for worldbuilding and quest design.

## The Archivist Core

### The Obsidian Keep (Core Capital)
- **Type:** Fortress/Citadel
- **Ruler:** The Archivist Council (7 members)
- **Key Features:**
  - The Record Halls (contains all historical documents)
  - The Induction Chambers (where citizens take oaths)
  - The Vaults (secrets of the Core's founding)
  - The Tower of Voices (communication with other regions)
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Infiltrate to steal historical records
  - Witness a Council trial
  - Access the forbidden Vaults

### The Grand Bazaar
- **Type:** Marketplace
- **Notable:**
  - Factions: Merchant Guilds, Black Market Brokers
  - Events: Daily auctions, weekly markets
  - NPCs: Old woman selling "genuine" artifacts
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Find specific rare item
  - Investigate counterfeit goods
  - Meet informant in back alley

### The Royal Academy
- **Type:** Library/Fortress
- **Notable:**
  - Contains forbidden magical texts
  - Sybilla's original workplace
  - Ancient artifacts on display
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Access forbidden sections
  - Study specific magic
  - Investigate the library fire (years ago)

### The Noble Quarter
- **Type:** Residential (Elite)
- **Notable:**
  - Isolde's family estate
  - Corrupt noble gatherings
  - Secret society meetings
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Noble invitation required
  - Investigate noble crimes
  - Party politics

### The Ironhold (Prison)
- **Type:** Prison/Fortress
- **Notable:**
  - Houses political prisoners
  - Former location of Bram's crew
  - Secret execution chambers
- **Quest Hooks:**
  - Break someone out
  - Interrogate prisoners
  - Find evidence of Core crimes
