## Main Story Quests

## Companion Personal Quests

### Isolde's Quest: "The Torvath Record"
**Requirements:** Isolde in party, Act 2+
**Solution Path:**
1. Visit Obsidian Keep archives
2. Find the massacre documents
3. Confront Isolde's father
4. Choose: Expose or protect

**Endings:**
- **Expose (Good):** Family disgraced, Isolde respects player. Unlock Redeemer path.
- **Protect (Evil):** Family survives, Isolde doubts player. Unlock Zealot path.
- **Destroy (Neutral):** Evidence gone, Isolde confused. No transformation path.

### Bram's Quest: "The Vulture's Heart"
**Requirements:** Act 2+, night time
**Solution Path:**
1. Find Bram's old crew
2. Learn about the heist
3. Choose: Inform or participate

**Endings:**
- **Inform Guards (Good):** Crew arrested, Bram loyalty +20. Unlock Spymaster path.
- **Join Heist (Evil):** Gold acquired, Bram loyalty +10. Unlock Contractor path.
- **Both (Neutral):** Half the gold, crew scattered. No transformation.

### Sybilla's Quest: "The Chronicler's Burden"
**Requirements:** Sybilla in party, Act 2+

## Side Quests

### "The Lost Child" (Core)
- Find missing child in Sump
- Options: Return to Core family / Keep hidden / Find new home

### "The Broken Pump" (Sump)
- Repair or replace pump
- Options: Repair (long) / Replace (expensive) / Sabotage (Sump hostile)

### "The Cursed Blade" (Core/Choke)
- Retrieve cursed weapon
- Options: Wield (corruption) / Destroy (dangerous) / Bury (safe)

### "The Choke's Trial" (Choke)
- Pass forest trial
- Options: Combat / Stealth / Wisdom / Nature magic

### "The Fallen Monk" (Zenith)
- Bury or investigate monk corpse
- Options: Bury (honor) / Investigate (clues) / Take artifacts (corruption)
