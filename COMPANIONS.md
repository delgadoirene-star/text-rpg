## Companion Mechanics (High Level)

- **Active Party:** Player + 3 companions
- **Reserve:** Unlimited
- **Swap:** At camps/safe locations
- **Loyalty:** 0–100 (high level concept only; exact breakpoints withheld)
- **Friendship:** 0–10 (bonuses scale with time/positive interactions)

For full details (spoilers), see the non-spoiler-light internal design notes.
